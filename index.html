Healing From Trauma Bonds Journal

by Priscilla Rodriguez

During the next 7 weeks, we will dive into a journey to learn more about yourself and identify areas to heal from past trauma that affects our day-to-day relationship with loved ones, family & friends. By taking 5-10 minutes a day, this journal will help you discover and identify those areas or behaviors that keep you from living a healthy and peaceful life. This journal will guide you to find yourself and evolve into a new and happier person.
